Meet Jordan Guthrie, RMT

Experience the transformative power of massage therapy. As a licensed massage therapist with nearly six years of experience and having personally overcome multiple sports injuries, I understand the challenges of recovery firsthand. I know what it takes to get back to doing what you love. My passion is helping others achieve real, lasting results – just like I did. Let me help you on your journey to recovery and a better quality of life. Book your appointment today and discover the difference.

Jordan is registered with the Certified Registered Massage Therapist Association (CRMTA), with a degree in Massage Therapy from MaKami College Edmonton and specializes in Deep Tissue, Thai Massage, and Advanced Neuromuscular Facilitation.
